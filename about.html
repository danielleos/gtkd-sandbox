About Us

We aim to promote the Art of Taekwon-Do in Greenwich, East London under the England Taekwon-Do (ETA) Association which is affiliated to the International Taekwon-Do Federation.

Our School runs weekly classes for both adults and childen with instructors Mr Tran (III Degree) and Mr Rose-Trespeuch (III Degree).

Students at Greenwich Taekwon-Do range from beginners to advanced and new members are always welcome. The first lesson is free of charge, so please come along for your opportunity to try an introductory class in this highly effective self defence.

Get In Touch

Training

Taekwon-Do classes are generally 1-1½ hours in duration. Special training classes and seminars may be longer. The classes are generally split into 2 or 3 phases with the first phase almost always being a warm-up/fitness regime.

Clothing should be loose-fitting and stretchable - for example tracksuit bottoms and T-shirt - no footwear is required.

Training may be pad-work, patterns, technical kicking, blocking, weapon-defence, grappling, sparring and various other types of training to develop technique, accuracy, speed and power.

Pricing

Our training fees are as follows:

  • Children
    • £23/month to train with us once a week
    • £40/month to train with us twice a week
  • Adults
    • £30/month to train with us once a week
    • £60/month to train with us twice a week
Please contact us for more info.

In addition to this there is a £45 annual fee which permits you to train as part of the ETA, allowing you to attend seminars and workshops with many of the other clubs affiliated with the ETA.

If you pay to train twice a week with us, you are able to attend classes run by Master Denis (XIII Degree) every week with our sister school, Docklands Taekwondo Club (up to five extra sessions per week) as well as our monthly sparring sessions at no additional cost. If you pay to train once a week with us, you are allowed to attend our monthly sparring sessions for a nominal fee of £5.
