UKTA English Open 2019

17 March 2019

All of our competitors who attended the UKTA English Open 2019 won medals in at least one category.

In the 18-36 male green belt/blue tag category, Ryan Watts won a bronze medal for sparring; in the 18-36 female blue belt/red tag category, Danielle O'Sullivan won a silver medal in patterns and also a bronze medal for sparring; and in the 36+ all grades category, Martin Bowes won a silver in sparring.

As always, congratulations to you all, Greenwich Taekwon-Do Club is exceptionally proud of you!

ETA Hoodies

8 January 2019

The official ETA hoodies are now available for ordering. They are £30 each and come in three different colours red, black and grey. These can be worn to official events such as tournaments, seminars and competitions.

The available sizes are Small (S), Medium (M) or Large (L). If anyone is interested or wants more information on sizing, please let Mr Tran know before the order submission deadline on the 20th January.

2019 Dates

2 January 2019

Just a reminder that the first classes start back this week on Thursday; 6:15pm for juniors, 7:30pm for adults.

Preliminary colour belt grading dates for this year are 23rd March, 22nd June, 21st September & 14th December.

Some upcoming competitions are the P.U.M.A International Open on the 16th February in Swindon, and the UKTA English Open on the 16th/17th March in Guilford.

Both are open to juniors and adults from 8th Kup and above. If you are a lower grade it is still well worth attending competitions to support the club and get used to them.

UKTA UK Open 2018

11 November 2018

We had 1 competitor, Martin Bowes, representing GTKD at the UKTA UK Open today in Guilford. We are pleased to announce that he managed to bring home a silver medal in the -80kg Veteran Sparring category!

This is a huge achievement as there were 314 competitors from 65 schools throughout the UK (including many international schools). We are extremely proud of this result. Congratulations once again to you Martin!

ITF England Championships 2018: Results

29 October 2018

We had 4 competitors representing GTKD at the ITF England Championships this Sunday 28th October. We are pleased to announce that we managed to bring home 5 medals!

In the under 14 year-old coloured belts category, Florie Nalepa won bronze medals in both of her events, patterns and sparring and Caspar Nalepa did a fantastic job in patterns as well. In the 18-36 year-old coloured belts category, Ryan Watts won a bronze medal in his sparring event, we are very proud as he did very well going up both a weight class and grade category! Also in the 18-36 coloured belts category, Danielle O'Sullivan came back with two gold medals in patterns and sparring.

We are very proud of our competitors as they all performed extremely well!
